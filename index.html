About First Chapter Consultancy

First Chapter is a business consultancy built around one idea: growth needs clarity and confidence, not paperwork and guesswork. We guide founders through every critical step of formation — reading the market, defining the right structure, and executing with precision.

With deep expertise in local and international business laws, procedures and regulatory frameworks, we simplify company formation from start to finish, aligning purpose with progress and turning ambition into a business that's actually open.

Our services include

  • PRO & Government Liaison Services
  • Bank Account Opening Assistance
  • Visa Processing
  • Business Strategy & Compliance Support
